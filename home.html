Sleepover Crew Apocalypse AU

This is my character for a story idea made by me and my friends.

Set in the current year, a group of friends must survive in the zombie apocalypse together. Lots of trauma, lots of shenanigans :P

My character is a young nurse who used to live in a campsite, tending to the injured and making sure all is safe from the outbreak. This is until the campsite is ambushed and burned to the ground, giving her no choice but to flee. On her way, she encounters a young girl who also ran away from the fire. Adopting the little girl as her own, they both must start from scracth for survival
